Rocky Butte Running Club

Welcome to the Rocky Butte Running Club website!

This website was created for the new running club centralized around North-East Portland. We are associated with x and are planning to host an annual event in the future.

The main goal of the club is to get families outside and running. The club regularly fundraises by volunteering at races. These funds pay for food for the members and to send highschool members to running camps like Steen's Mountain.

One of the most notible events we volunteer for every year is the Pacific Crest Triathlon and Weekend Sports Festival in Sunriver, Oregon. We set-up, maintain, and tear-down the transition 1 (Swim to Bike) zone at the Wickiup Reservior.
